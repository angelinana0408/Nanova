About Nanova

    • Facebook
    • Twitter
    • LinkedIn

    Nanova Inc. was founded in 2007 by a group of four dedicated researchers, two of whom are from the University of Missouri. Nanova Biomaterials, Inc. is the product of their vision. Established in 2013, NBI was created to focus on nanotechnology research and to use that research to manufacture dental consumables. Our mission is to continually improve our products beyond the industry standards with the help of our brilliant engineers and rigorous research. NBI boasts a highly qualified staff, the majority of whom hold MS or PhD degrees in Materials Science, Chemical Engineering, or Mechanical Engineering.

    NBI’s $1.5 million state of the art facility includes a research and development lab, multiple production areas, and office space. Though not a requirement for the production and packaging of class II medical devices, NBI also installed a clean room, signifying our dedication to producing dental consumables of the highest quality.

    PlasmaDent was established in 2015 as a subsidiary of Nanova, Inc. to develop additional high-quality dental products. All research, development, and manufacturing takes place in our facility in Columbia, Missouri. We are proud to manufacture all of our products in the USA.

About Nanova Environmental Inc.

    Nanova Environmental Inc. has been manufacturing the most accurate equipment for environmental monitoring. We are very proud of launching the first portable 2D gas chromatography. It can be easily used for field testing in industrial and general indoor air quality with excellent sensitivity for more than 50 VOCs.
